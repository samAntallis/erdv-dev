General date format

General date format is : yyyy-mm-dd'T'HH:MM[:ss.SSS] where
   yyyy is the year
   mm is the month
   dd is the day
   HH is the hour
   MM is the minute
   ss is the seconds
   SSS is the milli-seconds

exemple of valid date :
   2004-07-31T23:59
   2004-07-31T23:59:35:150
note that seconds and milli-seconds are not supported by most of date typed properties
